About Sustainable Eating

Health x 2

Diet not only impacts us but the environment as well

What we eat ultimately decides what impact is going to be put into the environment. In a correlative way, because we are part of the ecosystem, the humanity's choices right now decides the fate of the ecosystem itself through its control at the top of the ecosystem.

Current Status Quo

The current impacts of dietary choices to environment

Currently, because of North America and the global demand for low-cost food. A large amount of demand falls on to meat production and alternatives. Food production including growing crops, raising livestock, fishing, and transporting all that food to our plates is responsible for 20% to 30% of total global greenhouse gas emissions. Moreover, 33% of non-icy land on the planet is being used to grow our food. Cows, pigs, and other farm livestock in Europe are producing more greenhouse gases every year than all of the bloc's cars and vans put together
